    How to Play

    1. Each game shows a stat category (e.g., Pass Yards, PPG) and 5 rows.
    2. Each row has a team constraint (specific team, division, or conference) and a year range.
    3. Click "+ add player" to search for a player who played for that team during those years, then pick the season you think maximizes the stat.
    4. Your total score is the sum (or average) of all 5 players' stat values. Try to maximize it!
    5. After filling a row, tap it to see the top 5 best possible answers and how your pick compares.
    6. Use the era filter in the header to control how far back the year ranges go. Default is 2015+ — change it to All-Time for a harder challenge.
    7. When you complete all 5 rows, you'll see your score as a percentage of the best possible. 90%+ earns confetti!
    8. Hit "Share Results" to copy a shareable summary to your clipboard.
    9. Click "New Game" anytime for a fresh puzzle. No daily limits!
