    How to Play

    1. Each game shows a stat category (e.g., Pass Yards, PPG) and 5 rows.
    2. Each row has a team constraint (specific team, division, or conference) and a year range.
    3. Click "+ add player" to search for a player, then pick the season you think maximizes the stat.
    4. Your total score is the sum (or average) of all 5 players' stat values.
    5. After filling a row, click it to see the top 5 best possible answers and how yours compares.
    6. Click "New Game" anytime for a fresh puzzle. No daily limits!
